ABOUT US

Sharcom Automotives is a car dealership and spare shop incorporated in Kenya dealing with importation and sale of new and secondhand cars together with spare parts.At Sharcom Automotives we pride ourselves in offering quality and carefully sourced vehicles, guided by our core values and a burning desire to satisfy customer needs. Vehicles in our dealership and spares are sourced from Japan, Thailand, United Kingdom, Australia among other countries to ensure variety of units to meet market demand.

VISION

To be the number one dealer choice for individual and corporate buyers of Motor vehicles and spares in Kenya and beyond, as well as offering motor vehicle advisory services and technical or machanicle support to customers.

OUR SERVICES

Direct sale of brand new vehicles to both individuals and corporates.
Servising of vehicles.
Direct sale of vehicle spare parts.
Automatic and mechanical advisory services.
